Change Types Reference

Timing Context

Change types are evaluated at different stages of the order lifecycle:

  • Before NETO has invoiced: the warehouse hasn't dispatched yet; the row is in flight.
  • After NETO dispatch but before REX receival: items are in transit or awaiting receival.
  • After invoice has been received into REX: items have been physically received and quantities updated.
